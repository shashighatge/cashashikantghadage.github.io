Privacy Policy

How Shashikant Ghadage & Associates collects, uses and protects the information you share with us.

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Shashikant Ghadage & Associates, Chartered Accountants ("we", "our", "the Firm") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to provide, which may include:

  • Your name and mobile number (for example, to access our financial calculators).
  • Your email address, business/entity name and the details of your enquiry, when you submit a contact or enquiry form.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect any sensitive personal data through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide the professional services you request.
  • To contact you regarding your enquiry by phone, WhatsApp or email.
  • To keep an internal record of enquiries so we can follow up appropriately.

3. How Your Information Is Stored

Enquiries submitted through this website are recorded in a private, access-controlled spreadsheet and may also be sent to our WhatsApp Business line. Access is restricted to authorised personnel of the Firm.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We do not share it with third parties for marketing. Information may be processed using reputable service providers (such as Google) solely to operate this website and handle your enquiry.

5. Confidentiality

As Chartered Accountants, we handle all client information with strict confidentiality in accordance with the ICAI Code of Ethics and applicable data-protection laws.

6. Data Retention

We retain enquiry information only for as long as necessary to respond to and service your request, or as required to meet legal and professional obligations.

7. Cookies & Analytics

This website may use basic analytics to understand aggregate, anonymised usage (such as page visits) in order to improve the site. It does not use this information to personally identify you.

8. Your Choices

You may request that we update or delete the personal information you have submitted by contacting us using the details below.
