Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

What services does Shashikant Ghadage & Associates provide?
We provide GST registration and return filing, Income Tax filing and planning, statutory and tax audits, company and LLP registration, ROC compliance, accounting, and project finance / CMA data services.
Which areas do you serve?
We serve clients in Shirur, Ranjangaon MIDC, Supe MIDC, Shikrapur, Sanaswadi, Koregaon Bhima, Daund, Parner, Shrigonda, Kashti, Wagholi, Kharadi and across Pune.
Do you help with GST registration and return filing?
Yes. We handle GST registration, monthly and quarterly returns, annual returns, GSTR-2B reconciliation and notice responses.
Can you assist with project finance and CMA data for bank loans?
Yes. We prepare CMA data and project reports for term loans, working capital and MSME funding, and advise on Maharashtra PSI subsidy eligibility.
