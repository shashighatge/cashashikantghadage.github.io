CMA Data & Bank Finance Checklist

Key documentation typically required to prepare a Project Report for Term Loans, Working Capital (CC/OD), or Mudra funding.

Entity Proof: Udyam Registration, Shop Act, PAN, and Partnership Deed / MOA.
Financial History: 3 Years Audited Balance Sheets and Income Tax Returns (for existing businesses).
Capital Assessment: Quotations for new Plant & Machinery, and Civil Engineer's estimate for factory building.
Operational Permits: MPCB Consent to Establish, FSSAI (if food processing), and MIDC Lease Agreement.
Financial Projections: 5 to 7 years projected CMA (Credit Monitoring Arrangement) Data validating Debt-Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR).
Our Approach: We utilise technology-assisted tools including Excel Power Query and financial modelling techniques to prepare CMA reports. For enquiries about project finance advisory, please contact our office.
Important: The subsidy estimator above provides indicative figures based on the Maharashtra Package Scheme of Incentives. Actual eligibility depends on multiple factors including entity type, date of commencement, compliance with environmental norms, and government notifications in force at the time of application. We recommend obtaining a detailed professional assessment before making investment decisions. For assistance, please reach out to our office.
